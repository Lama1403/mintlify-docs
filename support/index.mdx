## Providing Feedback

We're always looking to improve the developer experience on Zilliqa 2.0. Here are some ways you can help:

- **Documentation Improvements**: If you find errors or gaps in the documentation, let us know
- **Tool Suggestions**: Share ideas for new developer tools or improvements to existing ones
- **User Experience**: Tell us about your development experience and any pain points you encounter
- **Community Content**: Share tutorials, examples, or other resources you've created

<Info>
Your feedback helps us prioritize improvements and build better tools for the entire Zilliqa developer community.
</Info>

## Response Times

While we strive to respond to all inquiries promptly, response times may vary based on the complexity of the issue and current team availability. For urgent technical issues, Discord is typically the fastest way to get assistance.
